The Crystal Collector

Instructions

  • To begin, the computer will choose a random number goal.
  • The four crystals below will have a random fixed value associated with them for each round of the game.
  • When you click the crystal the value assigned will be added to the total.
  • Keep in mind that the values associated with the crystal are hidden from you, until you click on each crystal.
  • Make note of the values by watching how much the total increases.
  • If the total values of your clicks are equal to the random computer guess, you win.
  • If the total value of your clicks exceeds random computer guess, you lose.
  • NOTE: The crystals assigned value resets everytime the game restarts.
